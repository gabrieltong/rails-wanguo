About the Course

Value-Based Care: Making the Most of the Affordable Care Act provides essential information about patient engagement, preventive health visits and reimbursement procedures, and answers the following questions:

  • What are the types of questions you can ask to engage patients in setting and achieving health goals?
  • What are five main preventive health visit types and their required tasks?
  • How can you ensure that health visit tasks are documented accurately?
  • Where can you find up-to-date information for all types of preventive health visits?
  • What are critical provider resources to implement and accurately code preventive health visits?

Learning Objectives

The Value-Based Care: Making The Most of the Affordable Care Act course is designed to help you:

  • Increase patient engagement
  • Accurately execute and document preventive visits
  • Optimize reimbursement or relative value units (RVU) credits
